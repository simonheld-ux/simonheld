Contact form and email contact

If you use the contact form or send an email, I process the data you provide, in particular your name, email address and message, to respond to your enquiry. The legal basis is Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR where the enquiry relates to a contract or pre-contractual measures, and otherwise Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR based on my legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries.

The contact form is provided by Formcentric GmbH, Schaartor 1, 20459 Hamburg, Germany. When this page is opened, a JavaScript file is loaded from form.formcentric.com. Technically necessary connection data, in particular the IP address, may be transmitted to Formcentric.

Data entered in the form is transmitted to Formcentric, processed on servers in German data centres and forwarded to me. Formcentric processes this data on my behalf as a processor pursuant to Art. 28 GDPR. Further information is available in the Formcentric Privacy Policy.

Storage period

Enquiry data is deleted once it is no longer required to process the enquiry and no statutory retention obligations apply. Submissions stored in Formcentric are deleted or anonymised in accordance with the retention and deletion periods configured in the Formcentric account.

No analytics cookies and no tracking

This website does not currently use analytics cookies or web tracking. Formcentric may use only functionally necessary storage or security mechanisms to provide the contact form.
